Sonic Adventure 2 Speedruns

Sonic Adventure 2 Speedruns involve players completing the game as quickly as possible. This is achieved in videogames often by using techniques and glitches to minimize time finishing the game. The speedrunning community for Sonic Adventure 2 focuses on different categories, such as the most popular category "Hero Story" and subcategories like "180 Emblems" and "Any%". Players will often compete for world record times and engage in a community dedicated to mastering skills within the game. Speedrunning Sonic Adventure 2 showcases a different perspective on how to beat the game through community efforts to find new techniques and glitches to achieve the fastest possible completion times. Sonic Adventure 2 was also used as a reference for the live action Sonic the Hedgehog 3 film. [1]
